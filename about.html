About Us

Dimension Flux are pushing the envelope in producing high energy and bass fueled dance music, taking inspiration from House, Electro, DNB, and any other genre which takes their fancy. Sven and Angelina have extensive experience in both performance, sound design and recording, and together they embrace their diverse influences from various musical genres, creating a musical concoction that gets the brain synapses firing, and the feet moving.

Originally from Adelaide, Dimension Flux, have released originals and remixes over the years ranging from sassy, swung house, rock and funk styled breakbeat, drum n bass, and industrial electro, proving there is no style this duo cannot master. They have been in demand both in the UK and internationally for their unique remix style, having released remixes and originals on Alfa Matrix, KDB Breaks, Mizumo, and Kick It Records.
